Eligibility Requirements

  • Age: 18–65 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin: ≥ 12.5 g/dL
  • No donation in last 3 months
  • No major illness in last 6 months
  • Not pregnant or breastfeeding
