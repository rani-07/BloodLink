🩸 Our Story

Built to Save Lives,
One Drop at a Time

BloodLink was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: no one should lose their life because they couldn't find a blood donor in time.

Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Life and Death

Every 2 seconds, someone in India needs blood. Yet thousands of patients suffer because they can't connect with willing donors fast enough. BloodLink changes that.

We built a platform that makes finding a blood donor as simple as searching for a restaurant — fast, location-aware, and immediate. Our verified donor network spans 48 cities and is growing every day.

We believe that geography, time, and technology should never be barriers to saving a human life.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at BloodLink

🛡️

Privacy First

Donor information is never shared without explicit consent. Your privacy is sacred and we build every feature with that in mind.

⚡

Speed When It Matters

In emergencies, every minute counts. Our platform is optimized to connect seekers with donors within minutes, not hours.

✅

Verified & Trusted

All donors are verified through our multi-step process. Hospitals and seekers can rely on the accuracy of every profile.

🤝

Community Driven

BloodLink is powered by a community of generous donors. We celebrate every act of kindness and recognize our heroes.

🌍

Inclusive Access

Whether you're in a metro city or a tier-3 town, BloodLink works for you. We're committed to reaching every corner of India.

💡

Continuously Improving

We listen to our community and constantly evolve our platform to better serve donors, seekers, and healthcare providers.
